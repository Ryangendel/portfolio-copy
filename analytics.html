Pivot Tables & IF THAN ELSE

While working at Preferred Meals in Chicago, I was able to put my degree in economics in use to help develop a new national school lunch meal and commercial muffin. Using sales data from California I defined categories of different food choices and made three reports about the most common serving size of dishes sold, package quantity, and highest selling item within each group of foods. I also used IF THAN ELSE functions and many variations of pivot tables to create a report that thoroughly went over issues that were important. The middle report is below, feel free to check it out!
