Newscast Demo Reel

During my undergrad I tried many different career paths, and journalism is one of them. My contributions to the newscast helped MSU Denver to win two Regional Emmys for the Heartland Chapter. I wrote and created news segments that I wanted to see more in the news. Also, Within that time I operated most of the technical aspects of running a newscast - including: cameras, audio board, VTR, teleprompter, and more. This video is my demo reel. Although some of the camera angles and audio is not perfect on some of the packages, if you watch them chronologically you can see the improvements over time (the story on Bitcoin being the most recent).
